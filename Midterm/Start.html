Getting started

  1. Choose a quit space.
  2. Write at least 5 minutes a day. Consistency matters.
  3. Don't worry about grammar. Write freely.
  4. Try prompts when stuck

Daily journal prompts

  1. What made you smile today?
  2. What's something you're grateful for?
  3. Describe your dreams.
  4. What challenges did you overcome this week?
